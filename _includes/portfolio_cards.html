AllCollege needed a mobile app for their users to communicate with each other on the go. The app offers a news feed, a deals section, and a discussion board. You can talk and communicate with friends, and students at your university. Includes messaging capabilities, take & post photos, push notifications, and a beautiful interface, The AllCollege App offers a great community and seamless integration with the allcollege website.

Technologies behind the mobile app include Xamarin and C#. The website utilizes PHP & Joomla on the backend.

AllCollege can be downloaded at the app store and their website can be found at allcollege.us

SocialCryptoScore analyzes the social influcence of crypto currency and tries to predict the prices by charting social popularity. The application is built in Laravel, and uses various APIs (reddit, coinmarketcap, twitter).

Backend technologies include PHP-fpm, Nginx, and Amazon RDS. The deployment process is fully automated utilizing docker and terraform.

Frontend technologies include Webpack, ChartJS, Bootstrap, & Datatables
